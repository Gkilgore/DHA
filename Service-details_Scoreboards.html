Scoreboard Displays

At DHA Contracting, we specialize in installing scoreboard displays for various sports and applications. Our experienced team ensures a seamless installation process, delivering reliable and high-quality displays.

Types of Scoreboard Displays:

  • Indoor and Outdoor Scoreboards: We provide installations for all environments, ensuring visibility and functionality.
  • Sports-Specific Scoreboards: From soccer to swimming, basketball to baseball, we match your needs with top-quality displays from leading manufacturers.
  • Custom Solutions: We can connect you with manufacturers and help create tailored scoreboard solutions that meet the unique requirements of your facility and sport.

With extensive experience installing scoreboard displays throughout the northeast, we have a proven track record of successful projects. See our past projects!

Contact DHA Contracting today to discuss your scoreboard installation needs, and let our expert team bring your vision to life on the field or court.
