Case Study: Mount St. Mary Academy Scoreboard Upgrade

  • DHA Contracting Team
  • Jan 1, 2024

Project Overview

Mount St. Mary Academy, a prestigious Catholic high school in New Jersey, approached DHA Contracting to modernize their aging scoreboard system. The existing equipment was over 15 years old, with failing components and poor visibility that impacted the overall game experience for students, athletes, and spectators.

Challenges Faced

The original scoreboard system presented several issues:

  • Fading LED displays that were difficult to read during daytime games
  • Outdated control system requiring manual operation
  • Limited functionality with no shot clock or auxiliary displays
  • Frequent technical failures disrupting games
  • Inability to display real-time statistics or announcements

Solution Implemented

DHA Contracting designed and installed a comprehensive scoreboard upgrade that included:

  • Main Scoreboard: 12' x 8' LED display with 8,000 nit brightness
  • Shot Clock: Integrated wireless shot clock system
  • Control System: Modern touchscreen controller with wireless connectivity
  • Auxiliary Displays: Smaller scoreboards for additional court visibility
  • Sound System Integration: Connected public address system

Installation Process

The project was completed over a three-week period during the school's spring break to minimize disruption:

  1. Site Assessment: Detailed evaluation of existing infrastructure and power requirements
  2. System Design: Custom design to fit the gymnasium's architecture and lighting conditions
  3. Equipment Procurement: Sourcing high-quality components with appropriate warranties
  4. Installation: Professional mounting and electrical work
  5. Testing and Calibration: Comprehensive testing and color calibration
  6. Training: Staff training on operation and basic maintenance

Results and Impact

The upgrade transformed Mount St. Mary Academy's athletic facilities:

  • Improved Visibility: 300% increase in display brightness for better daytime readability
  • Enhanced Functionality: Added shot clocks, statistics display, and announcement capabilities
  • Reliability: Modern components reduced technical failures by 95%
  • Student Engagement: Better displays led to increased spectator participation
  • Professional Appearance: Modern equipment enhanced the school's reputation

"The new scoreboard system has exceeded our expectations. Not only is it more reliable, but it has really enhanced the game experience for our students and fans. The DHA team was professional, efficient, and delivered exactly what we needed."

— Athletic Director, Mount St. Mary Academy

Lessons Learned

This project highlighted several key considerations for educational institutions:

  • Plan upgrades during off-peak periods to minimize disruption
  • Invest in quality components that offer long-term reliability
  • Consider future expansion needs when designing new systems
  • Ensure comprehensive staff training for optimal system utilization

DHA Contracting continues to work with Mount St. Mary Academy on maintaining their new system and exploring future enhancements. This successful project demonstrates our commitment to delivering technology solutions that enhance educational environments and athletic experiences.
