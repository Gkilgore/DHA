Project Description

We are thrilled to showcase our recent installation of a Daktronics Football scoreboard at Union Highschool in NJ. This impressive scoreboard, manufactured by Daktronics, features a striking arch design that proudly displays the school's name and logo. With its advanced features and vibrant visuals, this scoreboard enhances the game experience for players, coaches, and spectators. We take pride in delivering top-quality installations that reflect the spirit and pride of the schools we serve.
