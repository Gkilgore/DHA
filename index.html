About Us

A family-owned construction company

specializing in the installation of scoreboards, videoboards, and message centers for schools, towns, and organizations. With a deep-rooted commitment to excellence and a passion for transforming spaces, we have been serving our community since 2018

A team of skilled professionals

DHA brings together expertise in construction, technology, and design to deliver exceptional results. We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that every installation meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Strong partnerships and customer satisfaction

At DHA Contracting, we believe that our success is built on strong partnerships and customer satisfaction. We take the time to understand our clients' unique needs, working collaboratively to design and implement customized solutions that exceed expectations. From concept to completion, we guide our clients through the entire process, providing transparent communication, expert advice, and timely project management.

Track record of successful installations

We are proud of our track record of successful installations, which have enhanced the experiences of schools, towns, and organizations across our region. Our commitment to innovation, reliability, and customer service sets us apart in the industry.
